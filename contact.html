Frequently Asked Questions

What size skip bin do I need for home renovation waste?

For small renovations, a 2-4m³ skip bin is usually enough for light waste such as timber, packaging, and household materials. Larger renovation projects typically require 6-8m³ or bigger depending on demolition volume. Choosing the correct size helps avoid overflow and additional collection costs.

How is skip bin pricing calculated in Wollongong?

Pricing is based on bin size, waste type, and disposal requirements. Green waste is generally more affordable, while construction and mixed waste may cost more due to processing and landfill fees. Selecting the right bin type from the beginning helps avoid unexpected costs.

What happens if I choose the wrong skip bin size?

If the bin is too small, you may face overflow issues or need a second collection. If it is too large, you may pay for unused space. That is why it is important to estimate your waste volume properly before booking.
